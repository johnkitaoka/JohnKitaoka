About Me

I'm currently working at AWS, where I'm focused on building and evaluating custom multimodal models and advising cloud customers on responsible AI workloads. Feel free to reach out!

My team is currently building safe and secure artificial intelligence solutions to help nonprofits, universities, and government entities. Previously, I developed software to automate business operations for a manufacturing firm, optimized a transit system for the city of Madison, and helped a startup implement marketing analytics as part of a venture capital fellowship. I have a background in computer science and mathematics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I worked as a teaching assistant for 3 years.
